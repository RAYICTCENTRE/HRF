Financial Accountability

Hope Revealed adheres to strict financial oversight. Quarterly reports are shared with community stakeholders and donors. We are registered with the Uganda NGO Bureau. All contributions are receipted and audited annually by independent auditors.

Certificate of Registration: NGO Bureau Reg. # UGANDA/2022/HR109 • All donations tax-deductible as per local regulations.

Our Commitment

Hope Revealed operates with full accountability to our beneficiaries, donors, and regulatory bodies. The board meets quarterly to review programs, finances, and strategic direction. We maintain open books and welcome inquiries from partners and supporters.
