Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer?

Fill out the contact form or visit our office. We welcome skilled and unskilled volunteers. You can also email volunteer@hoperevealed.org for current opportunities.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, we provide official receipts for all donations. Please consult your local tax advisor for applicable deductions. We are registered with the Uganda NGO Bureau.

How do I sponsor a program?

Email our Partnerships team at info@hoperevealedfoundation.org or call +256 786 033 088. We have sponsorship packages for education, livelihood training, and mission outreach.

Can I visit your projects?

Absolutely. We welcome visitors and short-term mission teams. Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to arrange a visit to our field locations.
