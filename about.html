About Hope Revealed

Rooted in faith, driven by compassion

Our Story

Hope Revealed is a Christian Community-Based Organization founded in 2022. We operate in Kiryandongo and Oyam Districts of Uganda to bring hope, empowerment, and transformation to disadvantaged communities, especially Christian groups facing economic and social marginalization.

What began as a small prayer group has grown into a movement of change — reaching thousands through mission outreach, health support, agricultural training, and youth sports.

🌟 Vision

To reach out to disadvantaged communities through mission, sport, health, agriculture, and livelihood programs — reflecting Christ's love in action.

✝️ Mission

To develop Christians who are self-reliant and sustainable by promoting creativity, innovation, and empowerment in spiritual, social, and economic life.
