Our Programs

Holistic transformation through 6 strategic pillars

✝️ Mission Outreach

We conduct gospel outreach in prisons and schools, sharing hope, discipleship, and positive transformation. Monthly services reach 1,200+ inmates and students.

⚽ Sports

Sports tournaments unite youth and communities while serving as a platform for gospel outreach and talent development. Annual Unity Cup draws 20+ teams.

🩺 Health & Counselling

We offer counseling and guidance for people living with HIV and chronic illnesses, focusing on holistic wellbeing. Free testing events quarterly.

🌾 Agriculture

Training in modern farming techniques helps families increase yields, fight poverty, and achieve food security. Demonstration farms established.

💼 Livelihood

Vocational skills training (tailoring, carpentry) and small-scale projects empower Christians to become financially independent.

📚 Capacity Building

We strengthen leaders, pastors, and community members with training in governance, ministry, and management. Leadership summits twice a year.
