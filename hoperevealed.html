About Us

Hope Revealed is a Christian Community-Based Organization founded in 2022. We operate in Kiryandongo and Oyam Districts of Uganda to bring hope, empowerment, and transformation to disadvantaged communities.

Vision: To reach out to disadvantaged communities through mission, sport, health, agriculture, and livelihood programs.

Mission: To develop Christians who are self-reliant and sustainable by promoting creativity, innovation, and empowerment in spiritual, social, and economic life.

Our Programs

Mission Outreach

We conduct gospel outreach in prisons and schools, sharing hope, discipleship, and positive transformation.

Sports

Sports tournaments unite youth and communities while serving as a platform for gospel outreach and talent development.

Health

We offer counseling and guidance for people living with HIV and chronic illnesses, focusing on holistic wellbeing.

Agriculture

Training in modern farming techniques helps families increase yields, fight poverty, and achieve food security.

Livelihood

Vocational skills training and small-scale projects empower Christians to become financially independent.

Capacity Building

We strengthen leaders, pastors, and community members with training in governance, ministry, and management.
